Aprenda Inglês Sozinho!

É completamente possível aprender inglês sozinho, utilizando blogs e páginas gratuítas na Internet. A escrita, o audio e a conversação na língua inglesa podem ser aprendidos e exercitados a partir de jogos, textos, podcasts, videos no Youtube e músicas que ensinam o idioma. Existem diversas formas e técnicas para praticar, estudar e aumentar a sua proficiência em qualquer idioma, tudo isso online, de forma gratuita e sem sair de casa.

Cronograma de Estudos

Ordem Cronológica

Basic 1 - Simple Present

Personal and professional presentation:

Personal Pronouns; Verb To Be; Verb to have; phrases on affirmative, negative and interrogative; basic adjectives; talk about feelings, physical appearance, routine with other basic verbs; 3rd person of singular; professions, nationality, time, days of the week, months, seasons, numbers, family members, fruits, clothes and colors; demonstrative, adjective and possessive pronouns; possessive with ‘s; do and does; Question words; Present Continuous; basic prepositions; plural of nouns,; articles; basic intensifiers; quantifiers; modals(can, could, would);

Basic 2 - Simple Past

Personal and professional presentation in the past:

Talk about yourself and routine in the past; regular and Irregular Verbs; did; tag questions; Past Continuous; idioms; Furniture and office vocabulary; false cognates; modals( ; time expressions; whether; hobbies; Indefinite Pronouns; directions; if clause; Parts of the Body; Health & Diseases;
